Electric Vehicle (EV) battery swapping is an innovative technology that allows EV owners to quickly replace their depleted batteries with fully charged ones at specialized swap stations...

What is Battery Swapping?

Battery swapping allows quick replacement of depleted EV batteries with fully charged ones, drastically reducing wait time and enabling 24/7 mobility.

How Does It Work?

  • Drive into a battery swap station
  • The old battery is automatically removed
  • A fresh, charged battery is inserted
  • You're back on the road within minutes
